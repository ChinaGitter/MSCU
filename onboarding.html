Select your job role and your region to access activities tailored for you. Use these activities to learn about your job role and related processes and tools to help you be successful at Microsoft.

Marketing

The Microsoft Marketing Academy provides a targeted training roadmap that can help you to develop and expand your skills and grow your marketing career potential.

Microsoft Marketing Academy provides you with exposure to world-class marketing experts, current marketing trends, and practical resources designed to give you a competitive edge, so you can:
  • Discover and apply insights and analysis to drive strategic, informed decisions
  • Connect emotionally and drive demand with modern storytelling
  • Deliver digital discipline with a purpose
Please contact Sales & Marketing Academy if you are a new Marketer and have not been contacted about enrollment.

http://portals/marketing/professional-development/Pages/Marketing-Academy.aspx

Enterprise Service

Microsoft Services University (MSSU) is the global onboarding program for new employees in the Services organization. The goal of MSSU is to accelerate the development of new Services professionals so they more quickly provide effective service to customers through Enterprise Strategy, Consulting and Support. MSSU aligns training resources and new hire experiences to help new hires:
  • Build and practice the skills that they need to perform the core activities of their role;
  • Quickly improve their customer facing skills to effectively interact with customers and partners; 
  • Connect to the people, resources, and communities that can help support them as they start in their new Services role.
https://campus.partners.extranet.microsoft.com/sites/campus/beready/mssu/default.aspx

CSS

CSS engineer entry level is L55 (undergraduates) or L56 (graduates) for campus hire. For industry hire, the entry level is no higher than L57. From L57 to L58, engineer needs to take SEE Incubation Program and pass the tests. Most new hires are support engineer. In the New Hire Handbook, it lists different roles.

Sales

As a skill-based training program all year around, China Sales & Marketing Academy (CSMA) aims to help sales persons of Microsoft to make success rapidly by improving the consultative selling skills. The specific goals of CSMA include:
  • Build trust through in-depth understanding demand of customers/partners (Customer publicity)
  • Help customers to understand the value of Microsoft through effective communication, thereby improve the market shares (Microsoft advocacy)
  • Build world-class selling skills (Sales execution)
As a part of Microsoft training and Readiness course series, CSMA courses are customized to your role and focus on skills and technologies. When you complete the recommended courses and continue to practice and refine the skills acquired, CSMA will drive your career development and help you make personal and professional progress.
