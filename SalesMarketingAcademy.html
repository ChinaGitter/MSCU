About Sales & Marketing Academy

The Sales & Marketing Academy promotes practical learning skills to targeted skills sets that Microsoft is driving to help the sales team succeed in Microsoft China. Value selling proposition sets us up in China to better position the company in the market place and what value Microsoft can provide to our customers. We believe in learning from the class is important, learning from others is essential, most important learning on the job is critical to achieve our role of excellence. We encourage our sales to be more comprehensive than just knowing our products as today we are more pushing for value solution than just product sales.

Tyson Dowd

Dean of Sales & Marketing Academy

In the Cloud II

​​In the Cloud II results: SMS&P's In the Cloud Season II was launched mid-September after Season I. In the first round of the competition in November, 33 competitors and 12 teams stood out from 600+ SMSP sales staff in 4 tracks through rigorous selections.
​In the Cloud II results: SMS&P's In the Cloud Season II was launched mid-September after Season I. In the first round of the competition in November, 33 competitors and 12 teams stood out from 600+ SMSP sales staff in 4 tracks through rigorous selections.
• CAM-S Track: 8 competitors and 4 teams won out among 110 competitors.
• CTM Track: 18 competitors won out among 164 competitors.
• SMB Track: 7 competitors and 5 teams won out among 276 competitors.
• Partner Track: 12 competitors and 3 teams won out among 55 competitors.
Next step of In the Cloud II: In the coming events, the winning competitors / teams will present their solutions through videos, and the Track Champions will be selected by voting and participate in the GCR Cloud Competition on behalf of SMSP.
