About CSS Academy

The CSS Academy is designed to develop “Swiss Amy Knife” talents, or those who have deep knowledge in technical discipline but are able to collaborate across the boundaries of various disciplines, in order to drive excellent customer and partner experience and build customer loyalty to Microsoft. With tailor-made Readiness Roadmaps from New Hire to senior engineer, to Tech Lead, to CPR, CSS Academy provides a one-stop readiness platform and fast-track learning experience for engineers to grow from a freshman to GURU. We hope you can learn something in CSS Academy, but also LEARNING WITH FUN.

Anurag Vij

Dean of CSS Academy
