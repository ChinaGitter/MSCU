Director of Microsoft University

Jeffrey Krebs is Director of Microsoft China University since 2010, and is based in Beijing. Previously Jeff was Dean of the Engineering Academy and Director of Asia-Pacific Research & Development Engineering Excellence. Jeffrey established the Engineering Exellence team in China in 2006 in partnership with the Asia-Pacific R&D Senior Leadership Team to assist in the development of R&D engineering capability through expanded employee development and engineering system consulting programs.

Jeffrey has been with Microsoft since 1998, and came to Beijing in early 2006. He worked in Engineering Excellence for 7 years in various roles including global engineering discipline roles from headquarters. Prior to that, Jeffrey managed the localization of Exchange Server and SQL Server and other server products. Before Microsoft, Jeffrey worked in Japan and Seattle in both hardware and software development companies for both consumer and industrial applicatons. Throughout his career, Jeffrey has combined his passions for technology and its benefit to customers with a focus on international business.

Jeffrey is very happy living in Beijing with his wife, two daughters and small dog. He enjoys sampling the wide variety of Chinese and other Asian foods,hiking, and photography.
