About Partner Academy

Partner Academy is designed as a " One-stop Partner Readiness Platform" integrating Sales and Pre-Sales Technical trainings throughout partner individual and organizational growth path, Leveraging various online and offline trainings to enable partners to master the latest knowledge of products, technology and solutions, and to understand market trends and to develop high efficient sales skills so as to help partners to capture opportunities and win the market. As a “One Microsoft Partner Readiness Platform”, Partner Academy is endeavoring to enable Microsoft partners to improve capabilities and achieve business growth in China.

Dean of Partner Academy
