Partner Learning Center

Target Audience: All Microsoft Partner Network (MPN) Members
As a member of the Microsoft Partner Network, you have full access to a variety of PLC online courses, webcasts, virtual labs, and registration for instructor-led training programs and events to get up-to-speed on the latest products and technologies. Training is organized by exam, product, competency or solution and can be customized to meet your needs - no matter what size businesses you serve.

Opportunities include:

  • Technical training sessions, including product and solution based training for competency attainment
  • Professional development sessions for key business skills, including licensing and sales & marketing
  • Business assessments that satisfy Microsoft Partner Network requirements for licensing, and sales & marketing

How to get started:

  • If you haven’t already, enroll your company in the Microsoft Partner Network
  • Ensure that you have a Windows Live ID that is associated with your Microsoft Partner Network member organization
  • Sign in to the Partner Learning Center with your Windows Live ID.
  • Search and register for desired courses, or select a Partner Learning Path to get started

Partner sales enablement plan

Target Audience: All Microsoft partners
The Partner sales enablement program is designed to help partners increase knowledge and soft skills in selling Microsoft products. Based on Partner Academy portal courses, the program includes 4 levels from New Star, Specialist, Consultant, to Expert with statement of completion awarded for those who successfully pass each level.

One day partner bootcamp

Target Audience: Partners in Tier 2 cities covered by Microsoft City Expansion Plan

This training program serves for Microsoft City Expansion plan, and it provides to partners in the newly expanded Tier 2 cities the latest product and solution knowledge with high level selling skills and up-to-date Microsoft policy, strategy, promotion and incentive policy that are specific for business needs of Tier 2 cities, to enable partners to understand key selling capability and technical capability required to sell and deploy Microsoft products. After a one day Partner bootcamp , the Partner Academy will conduct hands-on labs to help partners attain practical selling capability necessary competencies for Partner technical deployment.

Schedule:

  • Offline training, focus on tier 2 cities covered by City Expansion plan
