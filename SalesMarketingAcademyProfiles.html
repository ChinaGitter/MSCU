Tyson Dowd

Dean of Sales & Marketing Academy

Tyson Dowd took on the role of Sales & Marketing Academy Dean and Area Readiness Manager and for Greater China SMSG since July 2nd FY13.
He has been with Microsoft for over nine years, and based in Asia for eight of those. In the past few years Tyson has been the APAC Area Compete Lead - leading share growth initiatives, and has been responsible for discerning key market and competitive trends, communicating them to customers, partners and Microsoft employees, and making recommendations on future product and strategy directions. Prior to his compete role Tyson was the DPE Lead for the Malaysian subsidiary, and managed Academic Evangelism in APAC and Australia, including driving programs such as Imagine Cup and Student Partner programs.
Tyson has been in the IT industry for 16 years and has enjoyed a variety of technical and business leadership positions dealing with business strategy, development platforms, government, partner and academic relationships.
Tyson loves to learn new things; understand different cultures & ideas and explore new cuisine. He and his wife have moved to Beijing.
