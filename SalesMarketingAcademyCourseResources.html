Learning Overview

Onboarding

The Sales & Marketing Academy delivers a roadmap of required trainings tailored to the major roles within the Sales and Tech Sales professions. These structured learning roadmaps bring a new employee to productivity by helping them understand how we sell at Microsoft, as well as understanding our business, products and services, and how we engage with our customers and partners.

Certification

Each sales employee is expected to earn Sales Profession and Discipline certification. This is achieved through completion of the required courses, knowledge assessments, and role playing assessment. This ensures that each seller has a baseline of skills and knowledge.

Ongoing Readiness

There are a wide range of programs and resources available to employees to support their ongoing readiness. On going delivery of online content such as FRI training including the Readiness Edge, the Out of the Box series for devices readiness, Gear Up (available in Chinese), and Infopedia all provide fresh and impactful information to help sellers be up to date.

The Pitch Perfect program helps all sellers prepare to have the key conversations with their customers that help with the transformation of our business and selling motion.

Targeted programs

The Sales & Marketing Academy also offers a range of programs targeted to specific groups or needs. The Marketing Academy provides the core learning for our marketing professionals.

ISU4U workshops deliver industry focused solution information in a more targeted way to sellers.

Compete clinics give sellers deep guidance on how to sell against competitors in their accounts.

Power Based Selling programs extend sales skills to have better conversations with customers how we can bring value to their business.

Creating Opportunity Through Account Planning (COTAP) workshops help our large account planners take a much more strategic approach to addressing potential of their enterprise customer accounts.
