Profiles

Anurag Vij

Dean of CSS Academy

Anurag Vij is the Senior Director, Global Business Support Greater China Area Lead, part of Customer Service and Support of Microsoft Services.

Anurag has led PFE for APAC, GCR & India, Global Partner Services Support and Cloud Service Support team. Anurag joined Microsoft in 2001, doing Technical Sales for EPG for South of India. Since then, Anurag has had the opportunity to move across several teams and geographies. He spent four years with Microsoft IT and in his last role with MS IT, Anurag was the Regional IT Director for Asia based out of Singapore. Anurag spent the next couple of years with Premier business running Premier Field Engineering organization in GCR, APAC and India. After that, Anurag moved to China to run Partner Services & Support and then moved on to build the Cloud support organization for what was then BPOS, now Office 365 and other online services.

Prior to Microsoft, Anurag was an IT infrastructure consultant and a certified BS 7799 Lead Auditor. Anurag holds a Degree in Electronics from Delhi University and a partially completed MBA from NTU, Singapore, which he hopes to complete one day when he get the chance back to Singapore.

In his spare time, Anurag tries to make progress on his not-so-good mandarin by practicing it with his two children and wife who are fantastic with the language already.
