How We Learn

Microsoft China University supports the learning and development needs of our employees across four disciplines. The University represents an integrated structure of four unique disciplines.

The learning and development needs of these disciplines are deployed through four Academies: the Leadership Academy, Sales & Marketing Academy, Partner Academy and CSS Academy. 

Each academy is overseen by an Academy Dean who partners with in business and corporate resources to ensure the design and implementation of relevant Academy curriculums for supporting the needs of the business and your professional development. 

We also work in an ongoing way to share best practices and fresh content across the academies to bring you a constantly improving set of programs for your success on the job and for your future career development. 

Your feedback is very important. Please direct any comments or questions pertaining to the University or offerings to askmscu@microsoft.com.

Jeffrey Krebs

Director of Microsoft University
