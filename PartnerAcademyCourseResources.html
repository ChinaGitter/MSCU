Learning Overview

Sales Specialist Program

The Sales Specialist program offers our partners structured learning paths and accreditations that bring them to readiness on our products, services, and licensing while also developing their Sales and Technical Pre-Sales capabilities.

Boot Camps

Each quarter, the Partner Academy hosts in-person Bootcamps that both help newer partner employees get needed skills for selling our products, services and solutions, and also help introduced partners to the latest information they need to be successful in the market. These Boot Camps are delivered in cities across China.

Webcasts

Webcasts provide a ready way for our partner-facing employees and BG teams to keep our partners current on products, promotions, licensing changes, and competitive information as well as build community engagement through online interaction. For currently scheduled webcasts, please visit the Sales Specialist Webcast site. Also, please see past webcasts at the replay site.

Soft Skills programs

The Partner Academy also schedules selling skills programs as well as general professional skills programs with targeted partners during the year to both address specific needs and to build value in relationship with top partners.
