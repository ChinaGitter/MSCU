Learning Overview

Onboarding Programs

New hire engineers participate in a detailed and structured roadmap suitable for their role and product area. This roadmap is assigned and tracked on the Readiness Portal. Please work with your manager to understand expectations, and visit the Readiness Portal to track your progress.

Learning Week Workshops

CSS Academy offers Learning Week workshops focused on priority products and technologies each year. For FY14, the Learning Week workshops will cover the following topics:
  • Private Cloud Foundation – Virtualization
  • SharePoint 2013
  • Exchange 2013
  • Lync 2013


Technical Training Resources

There is an extensive selection of training programs available that ensure you are prepared to deliver excellent support to our customers on our full range of products and services. Please see the CSS Academy programs page for full offerings list.

Premier Catalog Offerings

CSS Academy offers technology workshops and trainings for our customers and partners through the Premier Services catalog. Please visit http://cssacademy for the full catalog of offerings.

Online Technical resources

Please refer to MSDN and TechNet for the latest technical information and online community discussions about our products, services, and technologies.
