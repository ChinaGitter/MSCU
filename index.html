Welcome to Microsoft China University! We are delighted to continue to build and grow our programs to support the growth of Microsoft in China.

Microsoft China University is a unique investment by Microsoft in China, delivering learning programs to employees and partners in China to support your success. We work in partnership with Microsoft’s learning and readiness organizations, with our leaders here in China, and with all of you as both learners and subject-matter experts. We are here to help you take on the big, bold challenges before us all in China today.
