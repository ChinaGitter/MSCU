About Leadership Academy

The Leadership Academy is designed for our employees, managers and leaders in equipping them with the necessary skills, tools and knowledge to be effective in role. The Leadership Academy will focus on streamlining on boarding experiences and integrating existing development solutions. The Leadership Academy will build accountability and a consistent approach to employees, managers and leaders development across GCR in order to drive enhanced business performance and results.
