FY15 AP Tele O365 Workshop

​​The FY15 AP Tele O365 Workshop is a new type of training. It is an immersive industry sales scenario classroom experience, where trainees deal with real challenges from actual customers.

2015-3​

The FY15 AP Tele O365 Workshop is a new type of training. It is an immersive industry sales scenario classroom experience, where trainees deal with real challenges from actual customers. In the previous AP sales training, everyone recognized that applying what has been learned and combining theory with practice are extremely important for telemarketing. Short, but intensive and high-pressure courses will be extremely effective at standardizing sales practices. The essence of O365's all-new 3+L sales method lies in the process and coordination. The trainees will be grouped and allocated tasks according to their industries and roles. This allows each telesales to be integrated into a realistic scenario so that sales is a red line running through the entire process. From information gathering to the analysis of industry needs, from solution creation to customer communication, each sales person can arm themselves with copyright knowledge, experience the entire process of typical sales scenarios and go through comprehensive coach provided by the Microsoft APM team.

Azure University has been developed to enable partners and Microsoft employees in Sales roles to drive Microsoft Azure consumption.
We have Azure University: Solution Sales Track March 3-4, 2015 and Azure University: Technical Architecture Track March 5-6, 2015 in Shanghai. Participants regardless of their level of Azure experience, have been able to reinforce the Azure knowledge and contribute.
