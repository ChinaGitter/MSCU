About Leadership Academy

Employee Competency

Some competencies are core to an employee’s ability to get things done and be successful at Microsoft. The Leadership Academy offers powerful learning programs such as Precision Q&A, Crucial Conversations, and Decision Making & Problem Solving that help employees develop core competencies that will support their ability to deliver results at Microsoft.

Career Transitions

The Leadership Academy offers some programs to support key transitions of employees, such as becoming a new employee at Microsoft, or an individual contributor becoming a manager. Key program offerings are New Employee Orientation, and the Managing @ Microsoft series. These programs are designed to help employees understand the changing expectations, key results they need to deliver, and orient them to the resources and community they need to be successful in their new roles.

General Skills

Sometimes employees need to develop other skills to help them in their work. The Leadership Academy offers programs such as 7 Habits, Lateral Thinking, and language learning programs in English and Chinese.

Microsoft’s Rhythm of business

At key times of the year such as Mid-Year Check In and Annual Performance Review, the Leadership Academy offers programs to employees and managers to help them have more impactful conversations supporting career growth and performance processes.
