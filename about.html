Microsoft China University is your gateway to enhancing your professional and business knowledge, skills and capabilities to be successful today and in the future.

Microsoft China University helps you to grow in your career and develop your potential by bringing together best in class learning and development resources across professional skills and general employee skills in one integrated structure.

In partnership with business leadership, Microsoft China University demonstrates strong commitment by our leadership to your ongoing development, and seeks to foster a culture of continuous learning.

How we work

Microsoft China University supports the learning and development of our employees and partners in China. The University brings together the programs and expertise of several Academies: the Leadership Academy, Sales & Marketing Academy, Partner Academy, and the CSS Academy.

Each academy is led by an Academy Dean who partners with business and corporate resources and extends them to better support our needs here in China. Our goal is to deliver programs that address your needs in a clear and accessible way. We work to align all programs with business goals, so that you can achieve success with Microsoft.

Welcome to Microsoft's China University! Learn, grow, and achieve in your career by taking advantage of the learning and development resources available through our Academies.
