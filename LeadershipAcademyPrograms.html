Late Cancellation, No-Show, and Incomplete Policy:

Many Microsoft trainings have cancellation and no-show fees. (A no-show is someone who registers for a class but does not attend, cancellation after the deadline also occur costs.) These fees vary in the amount charged and, for cancellations, in the time requirement before a charge is incurred.

To avoid charges to your cost center, be sure to do the following:

· Read the Important Information section in the course registration details.
· Decline the training in advance of the cancellation deadline if you cannot attend.
· Sign the attendance form when attending the training.

Your goal is to learn. Our goal is to help by creating an ideal learning environment. By following the steps above, you help to ensure that learning effectiveness is increased with an ideal class size, that Microsoft training dollars are spent wisely, and that the maximum number of your coworkers can attend and augment their skills.

Please follow these policies to avoid a chargeback to your cost center. There are no exceptions to these policies.To learn more information please read our Late Cancellation, No-Show, and Incomplete Policy.
